Biography

Dr. Ke is an incoming Assistant Professor at the Ohio State University. We are a research group at the Ohio State University focuses on developing methodologies and utilizing bioinformatics tools, metagenomic sequencing, machine learning techniques, and animal models to understand the role of the human microbiome in chronic and infectious diseases. We are especially interested in how the human microbiome interect with pancreas and its role in pancreatitis and pancreatic cancer.
